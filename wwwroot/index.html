Setup on iOS

Go in "Settings" > "Wifi" > Click on the small "i" at the current wifi > "Configure DNS" > "Manual" > Remove all entries and add the IP from above > "Save" > Done!

Setup on Windows 11

Open "Settings" > "Network" > "Wifi" > Current Wifi > DNS-Attribution > "Manual" > Add IP from above at IPv4, remove others > Done!
